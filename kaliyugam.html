ABOUT

"Kaliyugam 2064" is a sci-fi thriller film set in a dystopian future where the world has been stripped of food, water, and humanity. It follows a group of individuals struggling for survival in a harsh, unforgiving environment. The story explores themes of survival, morality, and human resilience in the face of such adversity.
