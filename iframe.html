Agnyaathavaasi, directed by Trivikram Srinivas, is a 2018 Telugu-language action drama starring Pawan Kalyan, Keerthy Suresh, and Anu Emmanuel. The film follows Abhishikth Bhargav, an exiled heir who returns incognito to his father's company to investigate his father and brother's murders. Despite high expectations, the film received negative reviews for its weak screenplay and pacing, though Pawan Kalyan's performance and Anirudh Ravichander's music were appreciated. Controversy arose over alleged similarities to the French film Largo Winch, leading to legal issues. Ultimately, Agnyaathavaasi underperformed at the box office, recovering only about half of its investment.

The Ramayanam is one of the greatest ancient Indian epics, attributed to the sage Valmiki. It tells the story of Lord Rama, the prince of Ayodhya, who is the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. Rama is exiled to the forest for 14 years, accompanied by his wife Sita and brother Lakshmana. During exile, Sita is abducted by the demon king Ravana of Lanka. With the help of Hanuman and an army of monkeys, Rama defeats Ravana and rescues Sita. The epic emphasizes values like duty, honor, devotion, and righteousness, and continues to influence Hindu culture, art, and spirituality worldwide.
