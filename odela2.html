ABOUT

"Odela 2" is a supernatural thriller set in the village of Odela, Telangana, India. The film follows the return of Tirupathi, a man who was previously killed for his crimes, now returning as a vengeful spirit and continuing to terrorize the village. The villagers seek help from Bhairavi, a fierce Shiva Shakti, who arrives to confront Tirupathi's spirit and restore peace. The story explores themes of good versus evil, divine intervention, and the power of the supernatural.
