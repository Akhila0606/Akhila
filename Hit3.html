ABOUT

"HIT: The Third Case" follows a ruthless cop, Arjun Sarkar (Nani), who is assigned to investigate a series of brutal murders in Jammu & Kashmir. The case forces him to confront his own past and delves into the dark world of crime and justice. The film explores Arjun's psychological state as he navigates a complex investigation and grapples with the consequences of his actions.
